Private Balance Attestation

Compute your commitment hash offline. Your NSK and actual balance never leave this page.

1 — Compute commitment hash


    
Share only the commitment hash with the operator to include in the Merkle tree. NSK and balance stay private.

  


  

    
2 — Generate attestation proof

    
Once the operator publishes the Merkle root, use the CLI to generate your proof:

    
balance-attest prove \
  --nsk <your-nsk> \
  --program-owner <owner-hex> \
  --balance <N> \
  --threshold <T> \
  --context-id <gate-program-id> \
  --presenter-sk <your-ed25519-sk> \
  --merkle-root <root> \
  --leaf-index <N> \
  --merkle-path <sibling,sibling,...> \
  --out attest-receipt.bin

    
NSK and balance are private RISC0 inputs and do not appear in the proof output.

  


  

    
3 — Submit to gating program

    
Submit attest-receipt.bin to the on-chain gate instruction. The program verifies the receipt, checks the threshold, and marks the nullifier spent — one proof, one use.
